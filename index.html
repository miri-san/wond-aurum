Explore our facial aesthetic services

Microneedling

Microneedling stimulates the skins natural repair process to improve texture, tone, and elasticity. Using fine, controlled microchannels, we encourage collagen production for smoother, firmer, and more radiant skin all with minimal downtime.

PRF & PRP

PRF (Platelet-Rich Fibrin) and PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) harness your bodys own growth factors to restore vitality in both skin and soft tissue. Whether enhancing healing after dental procedures or rejuvenating the skin, these natural therapies deliver subtle, long-lasting renewal.

Advanced Skincare

At Aurum, we integrate advanced skincare backed by decades of scientific research. Our partnership with SkinCeuticals reflects our belief in precision, efficacy, and skin health, offering medical-grade formulas designed to protect, restore, and refine your complexion.

Botox & Fillers

Botox and fillers add balance and volume where you want it most lips, cheeks, or around the mouth. Whether used alone or alongside your smile treatment, fillers help create facial harmony and a rejuvenated, confident look.
